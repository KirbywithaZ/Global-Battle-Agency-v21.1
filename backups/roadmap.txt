Global Battle Agency / Full Roadmap

---

## **PHASE 1 — Online Storage (Basics)**

1. **Choose Pokémon to deposit**

   * Player can pick up to **5 Pokémon** to send to the cloud
   * Must leave at least **1 Pokémon in the party**
   * Show Pokémon info: species, level, shiny, etc.
   * Confirm before sending

2. **Choose Pokémon to withdraw**

   * Player can pick up to **5 Pokémon** to take from the cloud
   * Check party space before taking them
   * Show Pokémon info
   * Confirm before downloading

3. **Allow multiple Pokémon per player in the cloud**

   * Cloud can store more than one Pokémon
   * Make sure each Pokémon is separate and can be selected individually

4. **Set limits**

   * Maximum Pokémon stored in the cloud
   * Cooldowns so players can’t spam deposits or withdrawals

---

## **PHASE 2 — Mystery Gifts**

5. **Set up Mystery Gifts**

   * Store special Pokémon for players to claim
   * Gifts can be single-use, repeatable, or timed events

6. **Show available gifts**

   * Player can see what gifts they can claim
   * Show only basic info (species, level, shiny)

7. **Claim a gift**

   * Give the Pokémon to the player or store it in the cloud
   * Track which gifts were claimed

8. **Add rules**

   * Only claimable by certain game versions or progress
   * Expiry dates if the gift is temporary

---

## **PHASE 3 — Online Trading (Basics)**

9. **Set up trades**

   * Player A offers a Pokémon to trade
   * Player B can accept the trade
   * Swap Pokémon between players safely

10. **Cancel trades if needed**

    * Allow a player to cancel a trade if it doesn’t go through
    * Make sure no Pokémon get lost

---

## **PHASE 4 — Link Trading (Code-Based)**

11. **Create trade codes**

    * Player generates a short code for trading
    * Another player enters the code to connect

12. **Pick Pokémon for trade**

    * Both players select Pokémon to trade
    * Lock selections so they don’t change

13. **Confirm trade**

    * Both players confirm
    * Pokémon are exchanged

---

## **PHASE 5 — Wonder / Surprise Trade**

14. **Random trading**

    * Player deposits a Pokémon
    * Server randomly sends it to another player
    * Player receives a random Pokémon from someone else

15. **Check for fairness**

    * Make sure Pokémon are allowed (level, species, moves)
    * Reject broken or hacked Pokémon

16. **Show trade result**

    * Tell both players what Pokémon they got

---

## **PHASE 6 — Global Trade System (GTS)**

17. **List Pokémon for trade**

    * Player offers a Pokémon
    * Specify what Pokémon they want in return (species, level, shiny)

18. **Browse available trades**

    * Players can look through offers from others
    * Search and filter by species, level, shiny

19. **Execute a trade**

    * When two players match, swap Pokémon
    * Make sure both Pokémon are exchanged safely

---

## **PHASE 7 — Online Battling**

20. **Upload a team**

    * Save player’s team for online battles
    * Check that all Pokémon are legal

21. **Ghost battles**

    * Fight against saved teams controlled by AI
    * Works even if the opponent is offline

22. **Live battles**

    * Two players fight online in real time
    * Handle disconnects safely

23. **Battle types**

    * Single, Double, Triple battles
    * Multi-player battles (optional later)

---

## **PHASE 8 — Competitions**

24. **Ranked ladder**

    * Players gain ranks after battles
    * Seasons reset ranks periodically

25. **Tournaments**

    * Organize brackets for multiple players
    * Schedule matches automatically
    * Give rewards

26. **Battle replays**

    * Save battle logs so players can watch later

---

## **PHASE 9 — Security and Safety**

27. **Make data safe**

    * Prevent players from changing Pokémon data to cheat

28. **Prevent spam**

    * Limit how often players can deposit, withdraw, or trade

29. **Admin tools**

    * Monitor trades and battles
    * Fix problems if Pokémon disappear
    * Ban players if necessary
